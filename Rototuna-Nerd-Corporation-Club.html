Rototuna Nerd Corporation Club

A student-led technology club where members learn, build and contribute to real technology projects across the school.

About

How the Club Works

Members learn through hands-on projects, teamwork and mentoring. Everyone contributes to real technology initiatives while developing practical IT skills.

Activities

What We Do

  • Build and maintain Club
  • Refurbish laptops through the RAD programme
  • Learn computer hardware and troubleshooting
  • Coding and technology projects
  • Help with school technology initiatives
Meetings

Weekly Meetings

The club meets every Wednesday. Meetings include project work, planning, learning new skills and collaborating on current initiatives.

How to Join
